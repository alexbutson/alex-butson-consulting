About

"Most product leaders can strategize. Fewer can also run the operation behind it. Having done both — at enterprise scale — means I bring a perspective grounded in what actually works."

I've spent 15+ years at the intersection of product strategy and real-world execution — owning digital platforms end-to-end, from customer research and early roadmap through global launch and ongoing iteration.

My career has taken me from managing advertising technology at NBCUniversal supporting $450M in revenue, to owning a global B2B product suite deployed across 150+ events in 17 countries at Reed Exhibitions. I've led enterprise platform transformations at Shutterfly, built product operations at Informa, and consulted on global product initiatives at Mastercard.

I'm based in the New York metropolitan area and work with clients nationally and globally.
